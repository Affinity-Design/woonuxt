<objective>
Establish `/fr` route generation for the Canadian frontend without breaking existing English URLs, then mirror the current English caching and prerender behavior onto the French-prefixed routes.

Purpose: Phase 1 is the routing foundation for every later bilingual phase. If the locale contract, html lang output, and route rules are wrong here, all later text extraction, language switching, and SEO work will stack on a broken base.
Output: Routed French pages under `/fr`, dynamic `<html lang>`, and French-prefixed route rules/prerender inputs that match the English site behavior.
</objective>
